# About

This blog is a space to share technical knowledge alongside the philosophical questions that come up when you spend your days building systems and automating the world.

- **DevOps & Infrastructure** — thoughts on containers, CI/CD, and keeping things running
- **Philosophy** — reflections on technology, complexity, meaning, and the craft of building things
